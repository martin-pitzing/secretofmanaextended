# Visual Review Checklist

Use this when defining or reviewing chapter visuals, kits, benchmark rooms, or placeholder stages.

## Required Readability Checks

- readable hero locomotion
- committed attack animations
- enemy anticipation and recovery
- spatial combat lanes
- damage feedback
- camera support for combat
- ability selection flow
- party-state visibility
- strong enemy silhouettes
- clear interaction between melee, magic, and movement

## Expected Deliverable Shape

For a chapter visual profile or scene kit, try to state:

- palette target
- material or texture library
- must-have prop family
- second-pass prop family
- ambient and VFX notes
- Godot-first dressing targets
- do-not-do list

## Guardrails

- avoid remake-style gloss and HD-2D bloom overload
- avoid generic medieval clutter
- keep detail tied to ritual wear, social use, and regional function
- if final art is not ready, prefer in-engine still boards over empty fallback rooms
